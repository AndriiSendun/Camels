MANUFACTURING IN MONGOLIA

Mongolia's Gobi desert is a mysterious place of untoched beauty and hidden treasures.
Picturesque mountains, gushing rivers, sandy dunes and wide open country is the heart of the camel
land.
Extremely cold winters followed by intensely hot summers are characterized by he landscape and
its dwellers.
The Bactrian camel is perfectly equipped for such harsh climate. The soft luxurious hair of the camel
has been used for therapeutic clothing and household use for thousands of years.
100% designed in Canada, made in Mongolia. All Camel Heal line of products are manufactured by our sister company the Mongolia Heal
Company in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia.
Ever since our beginning in 2013, we have searched for quality Camel Wool Products, in 2018 we
were able to register a manufacturing company with Mongolia colleges to produce and export Camel
Heal Line of quality luxury therapeutic products.
The wool used in clothing is mainly camel hair and Yak wool from the Gobi Desert in Mongolia, the
wool is spun and dyed in Mongolia, with the highest quality garments. Machines are used to circular
knit the sleeves, the legs, and the body for best possible fit and comfort. The advantage of using
modern technology is that garments can be worn directly against the skin with minimum seams,
resulting in flexible and comfortable garments.
Once a garment is finished, its checked for highest quality finish and then it is packaged and distributed worldwide.
